Production Associate

Bensalem, PA - Manufacturing - Full-Time

Apply Now

SpecChem, LLC supplies and services the concrete distributor network with quality construction chemicals that provide performance and value to the industry.

We are seeking an intelligent, energetic candidate for a full-time Production Associate position in our Bensalem, PA facility.

The ideal candidate for this position has outstanding people and problem-solving skills, thinks innovatively, and constantly strives for excellence and improvement.

Responsibilities:

  • Package and stack finished manufactured product.
  • Clean and maintain work area.
  • Communicate with Lead Operator.
  • Turn off and lock out equipment when not in use.
  • Move waste from assembly line to dumpsters.
  • Operate within standard operating procedures.
  • Quality control - ensure that no damaged product is sent out and no damaged raw material accepted in.
  • Pacbkage finished product for shipping (shrink wrapping, boxing, labeling).
  • Stage finished product for loading.
  • Assist in other areas as needed.

Requirements:

  • High school diploma or GED
  • Ability to lift up to 75 pounds frequently
  • Ability to operate a forklift, or commitment to learn
  • Quick learner, team player
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Detail oriented and quality minded
  • Able to perform at a high level in a fast-paced environment
  • Have a passion for excellence - do great work
  • Prior experience in manufacturing is a plus
  • Ability to pass a background check, pre-employment physical exam, and drug screen

Why join SpecChem?

  • Industry leading compensation package and perks
  • Ability to make an impact and be rewarded for high performance
  • Comprehensive health and dental programs
  • 401k plan with company match
  • Work hard and play hard environment that gives back to its community
