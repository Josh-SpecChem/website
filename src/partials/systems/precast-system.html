PRECAST SYSTEM
  • 1

    Precast 80

    A chemically active, fast-drying release agent specially engineered to positively prevent bonding of the concrete to forms in precast and shop use applications.

  • 2

    Tricast

    TriAct is a concentrated, highly-reactive petroleum mixture that, when properly diluted, can be used as a reactive form release agent, an aluminum form "seasoning" agent, and a softener and maintenance coating.

  • 3

    Final Finish

    A single-component, rapid-hardening, polymer-modified concrete resurfacing mortar for smoothing, resurfacing, rubbing, and restoring concrete surfaces.

  • 4

    SpecPatch

    A general-purpose polymer-modified, concrete repair mortar for concrete floors, walls, precast, tilt-up, and masonry surfaces.
